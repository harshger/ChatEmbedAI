## German Market Override
Google Ads: sachliche Headlines, USP klar
Meta: DSGVO-konforme Zielgruppen-Hinweise
Keine irrefuehrenden Aussagen (UWG)
Preise mit MwSt.-Hinweis in Anzeigen
Keine Superlative ohne Belege
Abmahnrisiko: keine "Nr. 1" ohne Nachweis

# Ad Creative Skill

## Google Ads Format
- Headline 1 (30 chars): Main benefit
- Headline 2 (30 chars): Feature/USP
- Headline 3 (30 chars): CTA
- Description 1 (90 chars): Value proposition
- Description 2 (90 chars): Social proof or guarantee

## Meta Ads Format
- Primary text: Problem + Solution (125 chars visible)
- Headline: Clear benefit
- Description: Supporting detail
- CTA button: matching action

## German Legal Requirements
- No misleading claims (UWG compliance)
- Price must include MwSt. notice for B2C
- No unverified superlatives
- Competition references must be factual
- Landing page must match ad promise
