# Cold Email Skill

## Email Structure
1. Subject: specific, relevant, under 50 characters
2. Opening: personalized reference to recipient
3. Value proposition: one clear benefit
4. Social proof: brief mention of similar companies
5. CTA: low-commitment ask
6. Signature: professional with contact details

## Sequence Framework
- Email 1: Initial outreach with value
- Email 2 (3-5 days later): Follow-up with case study
- Email 3 (5-7 days later): Different angle or resource
- Email 4 (7 days later): Breakup email

## Best Practices
- Personalize every email
- Keep under 150 words
- One CTA per email
- Test subject lines
- Track open and reply rates
