# Competitor Alternatives Skill

## Page Structure
1. Introduction: Why people look for alternatives
2. Brief overview of the competitor
3. Your product overview
4. Feature comparison table
5. Pricing comparison
6. Use case scenarios
7. Customer testimonials
8. CTA to try your product

## Comparison Table Format
| Feature | Competitor | Your Product |
Keep factual, verifiable, no subjective claims.

## Legal Requirements (UWG)
- All claims must be verifiable
- No false statements about competitors
- No denigration of competitors
- Focus on factual differences
- Update regularly for accuracy

## SEO Strategy
- Target "[competitor] alternative" keywords
- Include related long-tail keywords
- Schema markup for comparison content
- Internal linking to feature pages
