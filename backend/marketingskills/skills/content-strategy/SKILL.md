# Content Strategy Skill

## Core Framework
- Define content pillars (3-5 themes)
- Map content to buyer journey stages
- Create editorial calendar with dates and channels
- Define content mix (educational, promotional, engagement)

## German Content Channels (Priority)
1. Xing - professional networking, long-form
2. LinkedIn - B2B, formal German
3. Company Blog - SEO, expertise
4. Instagram - visual, B2C only
5. Newsletter - direct, personal

## Calendar Format
Output as table: Date | Channel | Topic | Format | CTA | Notes

## Best Practices
- 60% educational, 20% promotional, 20% engagement
- Seasonal hooks: German holidays, trade fairs, budget planning cycles
- Repurpose content across channels
- Track performance metrics per channel
