# Customer Research Skill

## Research Methods
1. Review analysis (Google, Trustpilot)
2. Customer interviews (structured)
3. Surveys (GDPR-compliant)
4. NPS and CSAT tracking
5. Churn analysis

## German Review Platforms
- Google Reviews (most important for local)
- Trustpilot (well-known in DE)
- Kununu (employer reputation)
- ProvenExpert (service providers)
- Jameda (healthcare)

## Interview Framework
- Start formal ("Sie"-Form)
- Open-ended questions first
- Probe for specific examples
- Ask about decision process
- Thank with small incentive

## Survey Best Practices
- Max 10 questions
- GDPR consent at start
- Anonymous option
- Mix of scales and open text
- Mobile-optimized
