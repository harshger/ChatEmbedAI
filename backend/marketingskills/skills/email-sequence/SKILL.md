## German Email Sequence Override
Load german-market-context skill first.
Legal: comply with DSGVO Art. 6 and UWG §7
Double opt-in required before any marketing emails
Unsubscribe link mandatory in every email
Sender: use real name, not "Team ChatEmbed AI"
Tone: warm but formal ("Sie")
Sequence timing: longer gaps than US (3-5 days not 1-2)
Subject lines: no emojis, factual, under 50 chars

# Email Sequence Skill

## Sequence Types
1. Welcome/Onboarding (5-7 emails over 14 days)
2. Nurturing (ongoing, weekly)
3. Re-engagement (3 emails for inactive users)
4. Upsell (triggered by usage milestones)

## Onboarding Sequence Framework
- Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + quick start guide
- Email 2 (Day 1): Key feature tutorial
- Email 3 (Day 3): Success story / case study
- Email 4 (Day 5): Advanced tip
- Email 5 (Day 7): Check-in + support offer
- Email 6 (Day 10): Social proof + review ask
- Email 7 (Day 14): Upgrade prompt (if on free)

## Best Practices
- Segment by user behavior
- Personalize with user data
- Track open rates and click rates
- A/B test subject lines
- Include clear unsubscribe in every email
