## German Market Override
Deutsche Kunden reagieren besonders auf:
- Soziale Bewaehrtheit (Kundenzahlen, Logos)
- Autoritaet (Zertifikate, Auszeichnungen)
- Reziprozitaet (echte Gratis-Leistungen)
Deutsche reagieren NICHT gut auf:
- Kuenstliche Dringlichkeit (Misstrauen!)
- Uebertriebene Versprechungen
- Zu persoenliche Ansprache beim Erstkontakt

# Marketing Psychology Skill

## Key Principles for Germany
1. Social Proof - customer numbers, logos, testimonials
2. Authority - certifications, awards, expert endorsements
3. Reciprocity - genuine free value before asking
4. Scarcity - only if genuine (limited seats, beta access)
5. Loss aversion - what they miss without your product

## What NOT to Do in Germany
- Fake countdown timers
- Exaggerated claims
- Overly personal tone in first contact
- Pressure tactics ("Buy now or lose forever!")
- Fake reviews or testimonials

## Application Areas
- Pricing page optimization
- Email subject lines
- CTA button text
- Testimonial placement
- Onboarding flow design
