## German CRO Override
Load german-market-context skill first.
Trust signals for Germany: DSGVO-konform badge, EU-Server badge, Geld-zurück-Garantie, SSL-Zertifikat visible, real company address
Avoid: countdown timers, fake urgency, "Only 3 left!" tactics
German above-fold formula:
  H1: Clear benefit statement (no hype)
  H2: How it works in 1 sentence
  Social proof: German company names + cities
  CTA: Low-commitment ("Kostenlos testen")
  Trust bar: DSGVO | EU-Server | Keine Kreditkarte

# Page CRO Skill

## Conversion Optimization Framework
1. Analyze current page structure
2. Identify friction points
3. Prioritize by impact and effort
4. Suggest specific improvements

## Key Areas
- Above the fold: hero, headline, CTA
- Social proof placement
- Form optimization
- Trust signals
- Mobile experience
- Page speed impact on conversion
- CTA design and placement

## Testing Methodology
- A/B test one element at a time
- Run tests for at least 2 weeks
- Minimum sample size: 1000 visitors
- Focus on statistically significant results
