# RevOps Skill

## Process Optimization Areas
1. Lead capture and qualification
2. CRM setup and management
3. Sales pipeline stages
4. Follow-up automation
5. Reporting and forecasting

## CRM Recommendations for Germany
- Pipedrive (EU-hosted, popular in DE)
- HubSpot (EU server option)
- Zoho CRM (EU data center)
- Close.com (for small teams)

## GDPR Compliance for Sales
- Store consent proof for every lead
- Implement data retention policies
- Enable data export for leads
- Right to deletion workflow
- Document legal basis for processing

## Lead Scoring Model
- Demographic fit (industry, size, role)
- Behavioral signals (website visits, downloads)
- Engagement level (email opens, responses)
- Budget and timeline indicators
