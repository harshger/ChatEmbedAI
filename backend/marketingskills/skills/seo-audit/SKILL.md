# SEO Audit Skill

## On-Page SEO Checklist
- Title tags: unique, under 60 characters, include target keyword
- Meta descriptions: compelling, under 160 characters
- H1 tags: one per page, includes primary keyword
- URL structure: short, descriptive, includes keyword
- Image alt tags: descriptive, include keywords naturally
- Internal linking: link to relevant pages
- Content length: minimum 800 words for ranking pages

## Technical SEO
- Page speed: under 3 seconds load time
- Mobile responsiveness: Google Mobile-Friendly test
- SSL certificate: HTTPS required
- XML sitemap: submitted to Google Search Console
- Robots.txt: properly configured
- Canonical tags: prevent duplicate content

## Content SEO
- Target one primary keyword per page
- Use related keywords naturally
- Answer search intent completely
- Update content regularly
- Build topical authority with clusters

## Local SEO (German Market)
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Local keywords: "[Service] in [City]"
- German business directories: Das Örtliche, Gelbe Seiten
- Customer reviews on Google and Trustpilot
