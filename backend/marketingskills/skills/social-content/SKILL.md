# Social Content Skill

## Content Calendar Framework
- Monday: Industry insight / expertise
- Tuesday: Product tip / how-to
- Wednesday: Customer story / case study
- Thursday: Behind the scenes / team
- Friday: Weekly roundup / engagement post

## Post Formats
- Text-only thought leadership (200-300 words)
- Image + short caption (50-100 words)
- Carousel / document post (5-10 slides)
- Video (under 2 minutes)
- Poll (engagement driver)

## Best Practices
- Post consistently (3-5x per week)
- Engage with comments within 2 hours
- Use relevant hashtags (3-5 per post)
- Share industry news with your take
- Repurpose content across platforms
